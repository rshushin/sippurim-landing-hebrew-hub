Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We collect and store only the following data:

  • Your Telegram user ID (to unlock premium features);
  • Payment confirmation ID (to verify purchases).

No card or billing details pass through or are stored on our servers; payments are processed entirely by Paddle, PayPal or CryptoBot. We will never sell or share your data with third parties except when legally required.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@sippurim.com.
